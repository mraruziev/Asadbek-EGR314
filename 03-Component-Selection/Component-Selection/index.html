    Component Selection

    Module’ selected major components:

    Motors:
    I have decided to choose the 2nd option because it is relatively cheap compared to the first. Apart from that, a gearmotor can work with a lower voltage. I have used this motor on many of my projects, and it has never caused issues.

    Component Pros Cons
    FIT0489-D Gearmotor 40 RPM 12V turbo metal Price: $18.9 Works on hard surfaces Very nice torque for high load Low power consumption Very costly Heavier than most gearmotors and has very low RPM Need more power and hard to work with surface mount motor drivers
    Adafruit Gearmotor 200 RPM 3-6V DC Price: $2.95 Has high RPM Has special holes for mounting to the rover wheel Works well with lower voltage levels. Can’t always handle high load
    1528-2607-ND GEARMOTOR 120 RPM 3-6V BI-METAL Price: $4.5 Bi-metal gearbox Stronger than a simple gear motor Good price for its usage Low RPM Bit costly

    3.3 buck voltage regulator
    Final selection: LM2575
    Rationale:

    Component Pros Cons
    LM2575D2T-3.3R4G Price: $2.16 Very clear datasheet Good cost Up to 40V input voltage Has issues with Amperage Higher cost
    AP63203WU-7 Price: $0.71 Wide input range 2A output current Protection inside from thermal shutdown Noise of the switch
    UA78M33CDCY Price: $1.07 Low cost Only input and output needed Low efficiency with more than 6V supply
    LM2596S-3.3-ND Price: $8.76 Works well with 3A Input voltage operation up to 40 V Simple design for soldering Fixed at 150 kHz Too costly for my subsystem

    Motor driver. This motor driver will be used to make 4 of my motors move in different directions and to help control their speed. I am going to choose the 2nd option for my motor driver.

    Component Pros Cons
    BH6766FVM-GTR Price: $1.02 Suitable for small systems
    TLE9201SGAUMA1TR-ND Price: $3.77 Can use 2 motors Speed control with enable 1 and enable 2 Very high cost compared to the other 2 options
    IFX9201SGAUMA1 Price: $3.55 Simple design Short circuit protection Careful PCB layout Higher cost than many motor drivers Two devices would be required, so a bigger PCB space is needed

    Notes: All parts are from Digikey or other reliable part providers to ensure compatibility and availability of datasheet.
